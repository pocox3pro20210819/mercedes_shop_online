Model name: Mercedes-Maybach S-Class 580

Mercedes-Maybach S-Class 580 is the pinnacle of luxury and sophistication in the Mercedes-Benz family. It combines elegant design, advanced technologies and the highest level of comfort.

Exclusive appearance with refined lines and majestic proportions. High-quality materials are used in both the exterior and interior design.

The model is equipped with a powerful V8 engine that provides impressive power and smooth operation. It combines high performance with fuel efficiency.

In the Mercedes-Maybach S-Class 580, special attention is paid to passenger comfort. Large spacious salons with luxurious materials, innovative passive and active safety systems, as well as advanced entertainment technologies make traveling extremely comfortable.

Among the special technologies, it is possible to single out an intelligent control system, a new generation infotainment system, adaptive security systems and autonomy at the level, which allows the car to react to different road situations.
